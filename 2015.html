Events

  1. Elimination high card
    • Lowest card goes out in each round
    • At the end one sole Olympian is the points leader
  2. 100m dash
    • SHotgun your lane
  3. Bike TT race
    • Pah's bicycle will be used
    • No adjustment to the equipment
  4. Wrong arm throw
    • One attempt to throw football as far as possible
    • ...with the wrong arm
    • Stand where the football stops
  5. Jammie cup
    • One player in goal
    • All remaining Olympians score a goal to progress to the next round
    • Last reamaining Olympians in each round is eliminated
  6. Pint event
    • Straight race to finish a pint of beer or cider
    • Glass starts on the table and finished upside down
  7. Sporcle trivia blitz
    • Radomly assigned general knowledge test
    • 30 questions
    • In the event of a tie, a game of snap will determine the winner
  8. Worms
    • Randomly drawn semi-finals
    • Top two from each heat progress to final
    • Bottom two to battle for the few points that remain
  9. Fives
    • Standard rules apply
    • First out wins
    • Last remaining loses
  10. Regatta: Escape edition
    • Step 1
      • Down a can of K or special brew (6% minimum)
    • Step 2
      • Touch amenities building
    • Step 3
      • Eat three crackers on George Shipp lawn. Approved empty mouths signal event end
