The 2023 Leicester Olympic games

Dess code

No dress - an Olympics first

Location

Leicester City

Date

19th to 21st May

A host city like no other, Leicester held itself in contention as the greatest Olympics setting in history.

Excitement was palpable amongst the athletes, partially due to the new age format of no events list prior to the competition beginning. One by one as the athletes gathered speculation grew.

Sadly for Leicester, and sadly for the athletes and spectators alike, organisation will forever marr the occasion.
