Events

  1. Elimination high card
    • Lowest card goes out in each round
    • At the end one sole Olympian is the points leader
  2. Royal rumble
    • Last Olympian standing takes gold
    • When eliminated the controller is passed to the next competitor
  3. 100m dash relay
    • Split into two teams, each participant will run 100m
    • Run on standard athletics track
    • The baton will be a deflated condom
    • Captain will run additional leg, in the event on uneven teams
  4. Wrong arm throw
    • One attempt to launch a football as far as they can
    • Stand where the ball stops
    • Furthest throw wins
  5. Knob sled
    • Time trial bike event
    • Floor is lava
    • Practicing prohibited
    • Fastest wins
  6. Jammie cup
    • One player in goal
    • All remaining Olympians score a goal to progress to the next round
    • Last reamaining Olympians in each round is eliminated
  7. Pint race
    • First to finish wins
    • Spillage is not accepted and leads to disqualification
  8. 10 chip poker
    • Big blind starts at 2 chips small blind is 1 chip
    • Both blinds increase by 1 every round
    • One competitor eliminated per round
  9. Fives
    • Standard rules apply
    • First out wins
    • Last remaining loses
  10. Regatta: Escape edition
    • Step 1
      • Down a can of K or special brew (6% minimum)
      • When finished, head down the stairs to floor 3
    • Step 2
      • Eat a crackers
      • head to floor 2, repeat to floor 1
    • Step 3
      • Escape the building to finish the Olympics

Rules

Leaders jersey

The current leader heading into each event must wear the Carlton Banks sweater vest with nipple cutouts. Wearing the vest is mandatory, as is wearing nothing underneath the vest.


Jokers

Each contestant has 1 joker to use in the event of their choice. The joker will double the points awarded for the event. If using your joker for a round it must be eloquently declare in a loud manner beforehand. The joker cannot be used on the regatta or the relay.


Double points finale

As the final round of the Olympics, double points will be awarded for the Regatta event.
