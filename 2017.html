Events

  1. Elimination high card
    • Lowest card goes out in each round
    • At the end one sole Olympian is the points leader
  2. Pub golf
    • Two teams, five holes
    • Team captains, first and last place from previous event
    • Captains will nominate a team member to tackle each hole
    • Every member must drink at least one hole
      • Hole 1 = Guinness
      • Hole 2 = Lager
      • Hole 3 = Strawpedo
      • Hole 4 = Large glass of red wine and jaeger bomb
      • Hole 5 = Lager + sambuca
    • Each hole has one winner
  3. Strawpedo elimination
    • Loser eliminated
    • Repeat until one Olympian remains
  4. Royal rumble
    • Last Olympian standing takes gold
    • When eliminated the controller is passed to the next competitor
  5. Jammie cup
    • One player in goal
    • All remaining Olympians score a goal to progress to the next round
    • Last reamaining Olympians in each round is eliminated
  6. Wrong arm throw - boules style
    • Throw a football as close to the laid marker
    • ... with the weak arm
    • Closest wins
  7. 100m beach dash
    • Run bare foot for 100m on a pebble beach
  8. Pint race
    • First to finish wins
    • Glass must finish upside down on the table
    • Spillage is not accepted and leads to disqualification
  9. Stop the clock
    • The clock starts
    • One by one competitors must stop the clock when they believe 59 seconds has passed
    • Most accurate wins
  10. Beer pong
    • In Pairs
    • Semi finals followed by third palce paly off and the final
    • Established beer pong rules apply
    • Teams drawn from opposite ends of the championship standings
  11. Regatta: Beach edition
    • Step 1
      • Down a can of K or special brew (7% minimum)
      • Next, Sprint along pebble beach and back
    • Step 2
      • Eat 3 crackers
    • Step 3
      • Entire body and head submerged in the sea
      • Finished
