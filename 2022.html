Events

  1. F 's fishbowl face fuck fury (team event)
    • The return of a classic
    • Finish your buckets first to reap the early bragging rights
  2. Combined card
    • High card with a twist
    • Two attempts to aggregate your highest total
    • Any players with the same aggregate must compete in an elimination round to decide your placing
  3. Wrong arm beer pong
    • Honesty is the best policy… except in K-Money’s eyes…
    • Knockout rounds in teams of two (with one three)
    • 8 minute time cap (If you fail to clear the table, team with the least amount of cups remaining are the winners)
  4. Wrong arm blindfold throw
    • The classic with a twist
    • Ball must go past the line to register a throw (0 points if you fail this)
    • Distance measured by independent judge… (Rich-Tea)
  5. The chipper (team event)
    • Teams of 2 (one of 3)
    • Max number of beers shot + Max number of burpees in 6 minutes
    • In the event of a tie, teams must go again and compete against each other to decide a winner and subsequent placing
  6. Handstand pint race
    • "Get it down.... you Zulu Warrior"
    • Finish against the clock to claim the title of ultimate chugger
    • Assistance may be provided if you're pathetically weak..
  7. Wrong foot jammie cup
    • Another return of a classic with a twist
    • Olympic leader immune for 1st round
    • You know the rest of the rules so get the fuck on with it and stop asking questions
  8. Stack cup elimination
    • Leader and 2nd place start the game opposite each other
    • Grab a cup from the middle and bounce a ball into their cup
    • Once the player has succeeded, they will pass the cup and ball to their right
    • If a player successfully sinks the ball into the cup on their first try, they may pass the cup and ball to anyone around the table
    • If you miss on your first try you can only pass the cup to your RIGHT
    • After a player sinks a ball and there is a player directly to the right of them with a cup. STACK THAT PLAYER
    • Stack your cup and ball into the other players cup before they can get their ball sunk. The STACKED player immediately passes the whole stack to the payer on the right and retrieves a new cup from the centre of the table. Play continues until all the cups have been taken from the centre of the table. Player with all the cups stacked infront is eliminated
  9. Donut challenge
    • Get on your knees, hands behind your back, and munch that daggling donut as fast as you can!!!
    • If the donut falls to the floor, best get lower and suck it up....
  10. 100m army crawl
    • All-out warfare in another classic with a twist
    • 2 waves where the top 2 progress to the final
    • The remaining 5 losers must pathetically battle it out to scrap for the remaining places
  11. The ultimate Regatta
    • Time to "Finish Him!" - 2 cans, 2 laps, 2 rounds of crackers
    • A true test of a champion
    • Down a can, run a lap, eat your crackers then go again
    • Finish line TBA where glory awaits you.
