Events

  1. Top golf
    • After the first game, the two top scoring competitors from each bay will head to bay one to play for the top four positions
    • The two bottom scoring competitors from each bay will head to bay two to play for the bottom four positions
  2. Pint race
    • The draw of cards will decide the drink:
      • Ace = Ale
      • 2 = Lager
      • 3 = Cider
      • 4 = Guinness
    • Spills/dregs = disqualification
  3. Wrong arm frisbee
    • Use your non-dominant arm to fling a frisbee as far as possible
    • Order dictated by the draw of cards, highest first, lowest last
    • Competitors must not step over the flinging line
  4. Balloon popping
    • Inflate a balloon as fast as possible
    • The timer stops when the balloon pops
    • Competitors may only hold the balloon at the teat with the thumb and forefinger of their dominant hand
    • Fastest time wins.
  5. You plank
    • Competitors must assume the plank position and hold it for as long as possible
    • Longest timed plank wins
  6. Endurance strawpaedo
    • First to the bottom of a 70Cl VK wins
    • Spills/dregs = disqualification
  7. Cemetary hill 100
    • We will have two heats, each heat with four competitors
    • After the first round, the two fastest competitors from each heat will race again for the top four positions
    • The two slowest competitors will race again for the bottom four positions
  8. Hammer and nail game
    • Competitors must drive a nail into a log using the narrow end of a cross pein hammer
    • First to drive the nail all the way into the log so that the head is flush with (or below) the surface of the log wins
  9. Fives
    • Standard rules apply
    • First out wins
    • Last remaining loses
  10. Non-tactical pub golf triathlon
    • Pub golf scoring
    • Highest number of points over 3 holes wins
      • -Pint of ale, bitter, lager, cider or Guinness = Par 2
      • Single spirit & mixer = Par 2
      • Double spirit & mixer = Par 3
      • Glass of wine = Par 3
      • Two of the same pint of ale, bitter, lager, cider or Guinness = Par 5
      • Two different pints of ale, bitter, lager, cider or Guinness = Par 6
      • Bottle of wine = par 10
    • Anyone caught chundering, either tactically or accidentally will be stripped of their points for this game (sorry Keith)
  11. Regatta
    • This year’s Regatta will be in reverse
    • The Regatta will be completed around a football pitch with the corners acting as bases
    • At bases 1, 2 and 3, competitors must eat a single dry cream cracker
    • They must present their empty mouth to the competitor behind them before continuing on to the next base
    • At the fourth and last base, the competitor must down a can of strong lager or cider (7%+) e.g. Special Brew or K. First to finish the can wins

Rules

Leaders jersey

The leaders Jersey this year will be the infamous hemp dress. Costing just 50p from a thrift shop we will certainly be getting our monies worth out of it this weekend. Starting on the Saturday morning, the leader must wear the hemp dress at all times. The hood must be up when competing but may be removed when not. Wear it with pride.


Losers jersey

This year there will also be a losers jersey in the form of a hi-vis jacket with the word ‘LOSER’ written across the back. Again, starting on the Saturday morning, the loser must wear the hi-vis jacket at all times. Hang your head in shame, loser.


Jokers

Standard joker rules apply. 1 joker per competitor. You must clearly announce that you wish to use your joker before the relevant round begins. Your joker will double your score for that round. Your Joker cannot be used for the Regatta.
